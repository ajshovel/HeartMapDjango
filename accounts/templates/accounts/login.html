D150 Fueling: Our Mission

We are a group of students striving to help those who suffer from heart arrhythmias and other underlying medical conditions pertaining to the heart.

Our Progress

Objectives

Our objective is to build software that can not only understand the types of heart arrhythmias that occur, but to also help predict issues to come. We plan on also implementing EEG signal analysis to conclude any and all correlations between brain waves and heart rates. We would ideally like to implement a live feed of the heart rates for our users who need this feature.
